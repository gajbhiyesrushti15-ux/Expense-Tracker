About

Ledger

Ledger is a lightweight personal expense tracker built to help you record daily income and expenses, watch your monthly budget, and see where your money actually goes.

  • All data is stored locally in your browser (Local Storage) — nothing is sent anywhere.
  • Add, edit, and delete transactions freely — deletions can be undone from the toast that appears.
  • Search, filter, and sort by category, type, date, or amount.
  • Track a monthly budget with a live progress bar.
  • Visualize category spending with an animated donut chart and a balance trend sparkline.
  • Switch between dark and light themes with the toggle in the header.
  • Export your full transaction history as a CSV file at any time.

Keyboard shortcuts: press N to jump to Add Transaction, / to focus search on Home.

Built with HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript.
